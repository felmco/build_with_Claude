# 5.1 Few-Shot Learning

Few-shot learning involves providing the model with a few examples of "Input -> Desired Output" pairs.

## Zero-Shot vs Few-Shot

**Zero-Shot:**
> "Classify this email: 'My refund is late.' -> ?"

**Few-Shot:**
> "Classify this email: 'I love this product!' -> Positive
> 'This is broken.' -> Negative
> 'My refund is late.' -> ?"

## When to use it?
- Classification tasks.
- Specific formatting requirements.
- Mimicking a specific tone or style.
